App Description

📷 Your Moments, Your Stories. Introducing Capture — a powerful yet simple gallery app that lets you save photos and videos with custom descriptions. Whether it's a birthday, a funny moment, or a random memory, you can add notes to help you remember exactly what happened. No more scrolling through your gallery wondering, “What was this about?” Now, every photo and video comes with context, emotion, and meaning.

Key Features

- 🖼️ Clean and easy-to-use gallery interface

- 📝 Add custom titles and descriptions to each photo or video

- 🔍 Search media by keywords from your descriptions
